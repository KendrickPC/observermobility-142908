All TERRAIN

The Observer works on roads which traditional wheelchairs simply can't. The Observer was engineered to handle rain, sand, mud, trails and much more.

ADJUSTING GRADIENTER EQUIPMENT

When driving up and down stairs or hills, the Observer will keep your body in the same upright position as if driving along a flat surface. The Observer makes driving more comfortable and safe. Stairs and steps are not a problem for the Observer.
